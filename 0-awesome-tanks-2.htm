Instructions

Press the left side of the screen to move your tank around. Hold the right side of the screen to shoot. Buy new weapons between levels, and then tap the icon to switch to them.

Move your tank with the arrow keys or WASD. Aim your tank and fire with your mouse. Defeat enemy tanks, collect coins, and destroy the enemy base in order to clear each level. Spend your money wisely to upgrade your tank with better armor, weapons, visibility, and more.

How many levels does Awesome Tanks 2 have?

Just like Awesome Tanks 1, Awesome Tanks 2 has 15 high-action levels for you to get through in order to achieve victory. Every level is completely unique, and none of the levels are just remakes of its predecessor. Its the same general game idea, but all new maps!

How is Awesome Tanks 2 different from Awesome Tanks 1?

Great question! We didnt want to stray too far from the first Awesome Tanks. After all, why change what works? However, as previously mentioned, we added 15 completely new maps for players to enjoy.

Along with this, there are a few new upgrades that we think players will enjoy. This is especially true in the weapons department, where we have 4 new devices of destruction for players to have some fun with. Dont worry though, we still have the original weapons. Weve just added in a few new ones, such as a flamethrower and some mines for some explosive gameplay.

What is the best weapon in Awesome Tanks 2?

Naturally, after hearing about all of the new upgrades, you probably have one question - what is the best weapon in Awesome Tanks 2 now? That answer is actually a little bit complicated, as different weapons serve different purposes. 

For giant hoards of oncoming tanks, the laser is perhaps the best due to its constant stream of damage. No need to conserve ammunition, just point at the attackers and watch them fall by the wayside.

However, the Railgun is slightly more powerful, yet there is limited ammo and players still need to be able to shoot the moving targets. If you have elite aim, the railgun is likely better. If you are still not an expert marksman, then the laser might better be suited for you. It really just comes down to preference. 

When did Awesome Tanks 2 come out?

Awesome Tanks 2 came out in 2012, soon after the first game of the series. After seeing the reaction of fans, the creator got right to working on Awesome Tanks 2, which was released on many popular computer games websites roughly a decade ago.
